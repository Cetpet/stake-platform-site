How This System Works

Purpose

This system offers a transparent alternative to trading cryptocurrencies, stocks, and other instruments. It allows you to participate in a system with fixed stakes, known risks, and predictable profits.

How It Works

  1. Select a Group: Choose an active group (e.g., 1 beneficiary out of 2 participants).
  2. Stake: Provide a fixed amount in cryptocurrency (e.g., $100 equivalent).
  3. Wait for Participants: When all participants join, the system automatically allocates rewards.
  4. Receive Rewards: Beneficiaries receive their share of the total pool, minus minimal operational costs.
